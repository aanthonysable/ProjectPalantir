# ADR 0001 - Use Project Palantir as Internal Codename

## Status
Accepted

## Context
The team wanted a codename inspired by Lord of the Rings or Star Wars. Palantir strongly communicates shared vision, knowledge, communication, and coordination. However, Palantir Technologies is a well-known data/AI company.

## Decision
Use **Project Palantir** as an internal codename only. Do not treat the name as a commercial brand without legal review.

## Consequences
- The internal team gets a memorable codename.
- If the system is commercialized, naming should be revisited.
